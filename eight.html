Trigonometry

trigonometry have 6 different formula: sin, cos, tan, csc, sec, cot.

Explanation


Sin: Sin stand for sine, which is saying that a sine of a triangle is equal to the opposite side over the hypotenus, you will need to you know to calculate the other formula and to calculate triangles problem


Cos: this stands for cosine, and it is equal to the adjacent over hypotenuse of a triangle.


tan: this stands for tangent, it is equal to the opposite over adjacent and another way you can find tangent is take sin and divide it by cos


Csc, Sec, and Cot: these are the opposite of Sin, Cosine, and Tangent. Csc is the opposite of cosine, which is hypotenuse over opposite. Sec is the oppo of cosine which is hypotenuse over adjacent. And cot is the opposite of tangent which is Adjacent over opposite
